* GET YOUR MADELIA PACK *


Once again this Christmas, Primavera Sound will offer its Primavera Pack, a special limited edition box of only 1000 units that contains exclusive gifts and special advantages for the next edition of the Barcelona festival.

Each Primavera Pack contains:

- A Primavera Sound Barcelona 2016 full festival ticket with the following advantages:
Preferential access to the concerts programmed in the Auditori Rockdelux in the Parc del Fòrum and preferential access to the Primavera a la Ciutat programme (until venue is full and except in the case of those for which a reservation ticket is necessary)
- Official Primavera Sound 2016 Book (voucher to exchange at the official Primavera Sound merchandising stand during the festival)
- A 5€ discount on Primavera Sound 2016 merchandising at the official merchandising stand and in La Botiga del Primavera Sound (c/Ases, 1, Barcelona). Valid for purchases of 10€ and over.
- Tote bag with Primavera Sound 2016 image
- Special edition Primavera Sound 2016 notebook
- Primavera Sound 2016 mug
- Primavera Sound 2016 stickers

Also, 10 Primavera Packs will contain Silver Penny cards (one invitation for Primavera Sound Barcelona 2017) and one Primavera Pack will contain a Golden Penny card (pass with VIP access for two people during 5 years to all the festivals, tours and concerts organised by Primavera Sound with no exceptions, valid until 31/12/2020).

Pre-sale packs now available at the price of 165€ on Ticketmaster and the Primavera Sound Portal. From the 9th December the Packs will be available at the same price in La Botiga del Primavera Sound in Barcelona (Ases, 1).


* Available until the 7th January (inclusive) or until they sell out
* The postage of the packs bought on pre-sale will start on the 9th December
