* GET YOUR MADELIA PACK *


Once again this Easter, MADELIA Sound will offer its MADELIA Pack, a special limited edition box of only 1000 units that contains exclusive gifts and special advantages for the next edition of the festival.

Each MADELIA Pack contains:

- A MADELIA Sound 2016 full festival ticket with the following advantages:
Preferential access to the concerts programmed in the Auditori Rockdelux
- Official MADELIA Sound 2016 Book (voucher to exchange at the official MADELIA Sound merchandising stand during the festival)
- A 5€ discount on MADELIA Sound 2016 merchandising at the official merchandising stand. Valid for purchases of 10€ and over.
- Tote bag with MADELIA Sound 2016 image
- Special edition MADELIA Sound 2016 notebook
- MADELIA Sound 2016 mug
- MADELIA Sound 2016 stickers

Also, 10 MADELIA Packs will contain Silver Penny cards (one invitation for MADELIA Sound 2017) and one MADELIA Pack will contain a Golden Penny card (pass with VIP access for two people during 5 years to all the festivals, tours and concerts organised by MADELIA Sound with no exceptions, valid until 31/12/2020).

Pre-sale packs now available at the price of 165€ on Ticketmaster.


* Available until the 7th May (inclusive) or until they sell out
