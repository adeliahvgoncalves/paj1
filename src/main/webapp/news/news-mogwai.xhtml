* MOGWAI E DINOSAUR JR TO PLAY MAdelia 2016 *


Mogwai have been confirmed as the final headliner of this year’s Green Man festival. The Scottish rockers, who released their seventh studio album ‘Central Belters’ in 2015, join Dinosaur jr. in the event, which takes place in Wales’ Brecon Beacons from August 17-19. There are also rumours that Josh Tillman "aka" Father John Misty, Hippo Campus, Lemonheads and Hot Snakes are soon to be confirmed.
