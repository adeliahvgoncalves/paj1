CAMPING SITE FACILITIES

The disabled customer campsite is available to customers who are unable to camp in the standard campsites or who need access to the specialist facilities we provide. The campsite is more spacious and has track laid down where possible to improve accessibility for wheelchair users and customer with mobility impairments. Limit You are welcome to camp with your friends however; we must limit this due to the restrictions on space. In addition to the disabled customer and their carer, we will permit three more friends in your group. Please ensure you make us aware should you wish to camp with your friends so they are provided with the appropriate wristbands on arrival. If your friend(s) cancel, please notify us so we can offer their space to another person. Failure to do so may result in you being charged for their space.
