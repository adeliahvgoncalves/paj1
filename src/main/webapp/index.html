TWO MORE BANDS CONFIRMED

Mogwai and Dinousaur jr. join the already pretty amazing line up of MAdelia Sound 2016.
Both bands have been rumoured to be present in this years edition, they will join the likes of Pearl Jam, Arcade Fire, Iron Maiden, Radiohead and others. More names are expected to be confirmed soon. Read more here.

GREG DULLI

“An Evening With Greg Dulli” is the name of this American artist’s new tour, during which he will perform tracks from his prolific past as well as some new songs. Dulli started in the music business fronting The Afghan Whigs, one of the key bands of the 90s. After the band split at the end of that decade, Greg formed The Twilight Singers and he collaborated with his close friend Mark Lanegan in The Gutter Twins.
